Regional Prosperity Index - An index to measure overall prosperity for a region, considering night light index, population data, and GDP, measured on a scale of 0-1
Groundwater Condition - The trend of the amount of groundwater in the region as observed from satellite data
Major Crops - Crops that are widely grown in a certain district
Kharif Season - Cropping season from June to October
Rabi Season - Cropping season from November to April
Hectare (ha) - An area measurement unit, 1 ha - 2.47 acres

Cropping Intensity - The number of crop cycles in a year, as identified using satellite images. For one cycle it is Once a Year, for two it is Twice a Year.
Irrigation Condition - Can be irrigated or rainfed, as identified using satellite images
Crop Performance - Agricultural performance of a crop, categorized as Good, Average, or Below Average
Overall Farm(s) Score - The overall SatScore is calculated based on the agricultural parameters of tthe farms that belong to a farmer. Seasonal Score (Kharif & Rabi) - Score reflecting the aspects of the performance of the crop(s) in a particular season in terms of agricultural as well as revenue potential and land performance over the past 3 years.
Base Score - Score reflecting the cropping intensity and irrigation aspects of the given land.
Ambient Temperature - The air temperature measured in a particular sub-district.
Mandi - Agricultural market where the sale and purchase of agricultural commodities is done.
Yield - The yield is a measurement of the quantity of a crop grown per unit area of land (kg/ha).
Crop Area - The total area in which a particular crop is identified using satellite analysis.
