FARM DATA GLOSSARY
REPORT SECTION FARM DATA FARM CONTENT DEscription
Consumer Farm Score Farm Score Farm Score The farm score is calculated based on the agricultural parameters of the farms that belong to a farmer. It comprises of a base score (based on irrigation condition and cropping intensity of farms) and seasonal scores for kharif & rabi seasons, each calculated based on the farming history in the past 3 years. Insight: Farm Score reflects Overall Productivity & Revenue potential of the Farm
Base Score Score reflecting the year round capabilities -currently a combination of cropping intensity and irrigation mapping. Insight: Base Score reflects well equiped irrigation system and better land utilization for crop cultivation at Farm.
Average Kharif /Rabi Score It reflects capacity of a farm/farmer based on agricultural performance in the kharif/Rabi season. Insight: Score reflects Productivity & revenue potential of the farm in Kharif/Rabi season
Consumer Farm Summary Irrigation Condition Irrigation Condition Agricultural water content masured by using multipspectral data to classify if a land parcel is irrigated or not. Insight: Higher crop yield in irrigated farms compare to non-irrigated farms
Irrigated Insight: Survey Nos equiped with Irrigation system e.g. Pump, Drip etc.
Non-Irrigated Insight: Survey Nos depend on rain for irrigation
Cropping Intensity Cropping Intensity Number of crop cycles in a year, as identified using satellite image. For one cycle it is Once in a Year, for two it is a twice in a year. Insight: Cropping Intensity denotes level of land utilization for crop cultivation.
Once in a Year Insight: Survey Nos having one cultivation cycle of crop production. Partial land utilization for crop production.
Twice in a Year Insight: Survey Nos having two cultivation cycle of crop production. Total land utilization for crop production.
None Insight: No crop cultivation in the farm. Land not utilized for farming.
Crops Grown Crops Grown Insight:Name of the crops grown in the farm in last one year
Consumer Farm Details Major Crops in region Major Crops in region Insight: Crops that are wildely grown in a certain unit area/region. Benchmark to verify the cropping pattern of the farm vis a vis the district.
Season Insight: To asses the seasonal cropping pattern in a year (Kharif & Rabi). Kharif Season is from June to October & Rabi Season is from November to April.
Crop Name Insight: Major crops cultivated at district level
Crop Area It is the total area for a particular crop grown in a district over the season. Insight: Area under major crops at district level
Hactare (Ha) An area measurement unit, 1 ha = 2.47 acre
Average Yield Average Yield/unit area of the major crops at distict level
